Rajpack is a India, Chennai based company engaged in design, printing, manufacturing & distributing wide range of packaging materials such as corrugated boxes, cardboard boxes etc.. We provide package design solutions to a wide array of industry and end user applications.

Our packaging and shipping supplies are manufactured with high-quality materials that can protect your contents in a safe way. Our corrugation and packaging includes wide variety of sizes, color, GSM and printed materials. We also support wide varieties like 1 Ply, 3 Ply, 5 Ply, 7Ply and 9 Ply Boxes. We provides high quality corrugated boxes with affordable prize compared to market rate.

Our Journey So Far We started at the year 2001 with rented space with around 5 skilled and unskilled staffs. We had a annual turnover of around 10 lakhs per annum. Now we are in our own space of around 10000.sq.ft with two floors and having around 15+ skilled and unskilled labours. We are currently making a turnover of around 50 lakhs per annum.
